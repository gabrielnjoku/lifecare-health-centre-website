Welcome

At LifeCare Health Centre, we are committed to providing premium, accessible, and patient-centered healthcare. Our team of qualified professionals use modern technology to ensure you receive the best care possible. Whether it is a routine checkup or specialized treatment, we are here for you. LifeCare Health Centre focuses on outpatient care, providing essential health services such as routine check-ups, vaccinations, chronic disease management (e.g., diabetes, hypertension), and minor injury treatments. They also offer specialized care, including gynecology, dentistry, dermatology, and mental health services, often acting as a primary point of care before referrals

LifeCare Health Centre is a local clinic. It caters to patients' dental, mental health, substance use, and other health care needs. The Health Centre is community-based and led in part by patients. It offers services ranging from general consultations to specialized care (neurosurgery, cardiology) and digital health.

LifeCare Health Centre was founded in 2012 to address the gap between primary care and emergency rooms, offering immediate care with extended hours and no appointments. Starting with one location in Enugu, the Health Centre rapidly expanded to three locations by 2018, integrating onsite diagnostic imaging and laboratory services. In 2020, LifeCare Health Centre pivoted to include telemedicine options, focusing on convenience, rapid patient access, and digital-first health management to serve busy urban professionals and families.

Health Tips from Our Doctors

Small, consistent habits make the biggest difference to long-term health. Our clinicians share their top evidence-based advice for living well.

Heart Health Essentials

Cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of mortality worldwide. These practical steps can dramatically reduce your risk and improve overall heart function.

  1. Exercise for at least 30 minutes on most days of the week
  2. Reduce sodium intake to under 2,300 mg per day to protect blood pressure
  3. Quit smoking — even passive exposure significantly increases cardiovascular risk
  4. Monitor your blood pressure regularly at home or at the clinic
  5. Manage stress through mindfulness, light exercise, or meaningful social connection

Mental Wellbeing Every Day

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Building resilience and emotional awareness helps you navigate life's challenges with greater confidence and ease.

  1. Prioritise 7–9 hours of quality, uninterrupted sleep each night
  2. Maintain meaningful social relationships and active community ties
  3. Practice mindful breathing for 5–10 minutes daily to reduce cortisol levels
  4. Limit social media consumption, especially in the hour before bedtime
  5. Seek professional support early — there is no shame in speaking to a counsellor

Nutrition for Lifelong Vitality

A balanced, whole-foods diet is one of the most powerful and accessible tools for preventing chronic disease and maintaining sustained energy throughout the day.

  1. Fill half your plate with colourful vegetables and fresh fruits at every meal
  2. Choose whole grains over refined carbohydrates whenever possible
  3. Stay well-hydrated — aim for 8 glasses of water daily, more in hot climates
  4. Limit processed foods that are high in trans fats, added sugars, and preservatives
  5. Include lean proteins and healthy fats such as fish, legumes, nuts, and avocado
